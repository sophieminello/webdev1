Music

Samia released her debut album "The Baby" during quarantine 2020. Since then she's been non-stop, releasing a remix/cover album where fellow musicians would reimagine her songs. This year, she's released the Scout EP along with two other singles.

Concerts

Samia is currently touring the U.S. with a multitude of sold out dates! Crowd interaction is at an all time high. Before she has opened for acts like AJR and Hippo Campus, but for her first headlining tour she is establishing herself with her infectious stage presence.

About

Samia Finnerty is originally from New York City. Her parents are both actors. She has been in the music industry for quite a while but has just broken out as an indie act to keep a close eye on.
