About

Wow, what an awesome day in the orchard. Here, you’ll see our baskets of some of the freshest and the best music and art content. We are here to create a cozy environment where we can spin all our favorite records while admiring the artwork and greenery around us… Created in 2017, Sunlight Magazine was a high school passion project for Sophie Minello. Through Sunlight, she aimed to give both musicians and artists a platform to share any sort of content they felt passionate about. Since then, it has evolved beyond simply music, encapsulating a plethora of different topics. It has also expanded past the website format, printing 5 physical issues bi-yearly. Like all of us, Sunlight has gone through big changes since it’s birth, and it will always continue to grow. Thank you for choosing to grow with us. XOXO - Sunlight Team
